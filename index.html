Services

Ekodelite catering and Events has been creating and serving delicious meals and party snacks to individuals and businesses in Nigeria for eight years now. We have developed a reputation for efficiency and excellence. We offer a varied menu of appetizers, salads, main courses, and desserts from which you can select to individualize meals for your guests. You are in charge. Simply tell us which items you would like, the quantity of each, and the services you want us to provide. Then we wll present you with our plan and total invoice two weeks before your event for your final approval. We handle food preparation, setup, and cleanup for you, so you can just enjoy the party!

    Packages

    Certainly! Catering packages can vary widely based on factors such as the type of event, the number of guests, menu selections, and additional services provided. Here are our basic and pro catering packages.

    Basic Catering package:

    Description: This package is suitable for small to medium-sized events with straightforward requirements. It includes essential food and beverage options with standard service.

    Menu Options:


  • Choice of one appetizer, one main course, and one dessert.
  • Non-alcoholic beverages (e.g., water, soft drinks, fruit juice).
  • Basic tableware and disposable utensils.
  • Standard buffet setup or plated service.

    • Services Included:


  • Delivery of food to the venue.
  • Setup of buffet or plated service area.
  • Basic staffing (servers to replenish buffet, clear plates, and assist guests if needed)
  • Cleanup of catering area (removal of food and basic cleanup).

    • Price Range:

    #5,000- #20,00 per plate (depending on menu selections and service level).

    Pro Catering Package

    Description: This package is designed for larger events or those requiring a higher level of service and customization. It offers a wider selection of gourmet food options, premium beverages, and enhanced service.

    Menu Options:

  • Choice of multiple appetizers, main courses, and desserts (including vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options).
  • Selection of premium beverages (e.g., specialty cocktails, wine pairings, artisanal sodas).
  • Customizable menu with chef's recommendations.
  • Upscale presentation and garnishes.

    • Service Included

  • Full-service catering staff, including chefs, servers, bartenders, and event coordinators.
  • Professional setup of buffet or plated service area, including decorative elements and floral arrangements.
  • Butler-passed hors d'oeuvres and live food stations (e.g., carving station, pasta station).
  • Wine pairing or specialty cocktail service.
  • Assistance with event planning, timeline management, and coordination with other vendors.
  • Thorough cleanup of catering area, including disposal of waste and recycling.

    • Price Range:

    #10,000- #45,000 per plate (depending on menu selections, service level, and event customization).
